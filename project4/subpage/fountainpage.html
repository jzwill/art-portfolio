Environmental Asset

I created a custom model from scratch for this project, UV Mapping it before porting it over to Mudbox to sculpt. Once sculpted, I brought it back into Maya and added custom materials created partially with the paint tool in Mudbox.

Water Fountain

A lot of the detail in this project came from bump maps and Mudbox sculpting. It's a bit obscured because of the metalness in my fountain, but I believe it turned out fairly well.
