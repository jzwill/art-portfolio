Prop Modeling

This project was focused on modeling props to be used in video games. I sculpted this model from the ground up: UV Mapping, applying materials, and creating textures for it.

Hammer Lantern

I created this hammer based on my own original design I sketched out beforehand. For this model, I tried focusing on keeping a lower poly count while retaining as much detail as possible. I also drew my own custom texture for the metal, and I think it turned out rather well.
